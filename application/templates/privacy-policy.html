The data we collect is the data you provide to us. This includes your name, email, phone number, Michigan umid, and post contents. This includes all information you enter while using our service.

We log usage data when you visit or otherwise use our services on our website and application. Our Services allow you to join class groups, make resource posts, and find other students in your classes. This list is not exhaustive and we may log additional data as needed.

We use log-ins, cookies, device information, and internet protocol (“IP”) addresses to identify you and log your use. Most web browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. You can reset your web browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. Please note, however, that certain features of the site and application may not work properly if you delete or disable cookies. Any cookies collected by VOID TECH do not and cannot infiltrate a user's hard drive to collect any information stored on the hard drive.
